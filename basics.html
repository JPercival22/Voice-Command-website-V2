The Headstock

This is called the headstock, it can be found at the top of the guitar. The main function of this is to house the tuning mechanisms that hold the strings in place at the, "head," of the guitar.

The Bridge

This is called the bridge, it's located at the bottom of the guitar. It's job is to keep the strings securely in place using the pegs shown here in metallic blue.
