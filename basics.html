Parts of the Guitar

This page will explain the parts of the guitar you will need to remember, if in doubt you could always revisit this page during the tutorial, don't worry it will become second nature to you in no time at all.
For all voice commands for this website, just say, "What to say" at any time to open the Help Box.

The Headstock

This is called the headstock, it can be found at the top of the guitar. The main function of this is to house the tuning mechanisms that hold the strings in place at the, "head," of the guitar.

The Bridge

This is called the bridge, it's located at the bottom of the guitar. It's job is to keep the strings securely in place using the pegs shown here in metallic blue.

Guitars are made out of wood and strung with either nylon or steel strings
