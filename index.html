Lesson's

Lesson 1: The Basics

Starting off can be tough! There are many things to learn, in your first lesson we discover how to hold, stand and sit with a guitar. What the different components do and more importantly how to tune your guitar.

Lesson 2: Frets

In Lesson two, we learn the fundamentals on what a fret board looks like, how to easily locate fret numbers, sound waves, positive finger positioning and finger posture whilst playing your guitar.

Lesson 3: The Chords

In lesson 3, we learn all about the different types of chords played. For example, the Open Chord, the Barre Chords and the Power Chords

Lesson 4: The Rhythm Guitar

In Lesson 4 we learn how to keep to a beat, timing, more on chords, finger numbers and we learn how to play our first song.

Lesson 5: The Lead Guitar

For Lesson 5, we discover when, how and why the Electric Guitar was invented, how to read lead guitar tab's and have a lesson on how to play a solo.

Playing the guitar is really healthy, it can help to manage your stress, enhance your memory, improve motor skills and will help you feel more able to cope with performing in life!
