Help Center

Hello, saying, "Open Help Center," at any time opens the help menu, were you will find list of voice commands to navigate through the site.

What to say What happens
Scroll Up Scroll's the page up
Scroll Down Scroll's the page down
Go to the Top Takes you to the top of the page
Go to the bottom Takes you to the bottom of the page
Go forward Goes forward a previously visited page
Go backwards returns to the previously visited page
Home Takes you to the Home Page
Open Basics Takes you to the first Basics Lesson
Open Basics Lesson Two Takes you to the second Basics Lesson
Open Fret's Takes you to the first Frets Lesson
Open Fret's Lesson Two Takes you to the second Fret's lesson on Finger positioning.
Open Chords Takes you to the Chords Lesson
The Chord's Lesson Two Takes you to the second Chord's Lesson on Power Chords
Open Rhythm Takes you to the Rhythm Guitar Lesson
Open Rhythm Lesson Two Takes you to the second Rhythm Guitar Lesson, where you will learn a song
Open Lead Takes you to the Lead Guitar Lesson
Open Lead Lesson Two Takes you to the second Lead Guitar Lesson, where you will learn how to read music

Say "close" to close the help center

Welcome

Hello, my name is Joseph Percival, and welcome to,"Guitar Lessons," a voice activated tutorial website. I graduated from Edge Hill University with a 1st Class BSc.(Hons) in Web Design and Web Development. This was my dissertation project covering UX Research, UI Design and Web Development.

Just say, "Open Help Center" at any time to open the voice command menu.

This site site aims to give you some general understanding on many guitar aesthetics, how to play the guitar and how to read guitar chords and tab's. Its aim is to make learning easier so you don't have to 'put the guitar down to turn the next page.'

Below are the links to the main pages for this tutorial, if a lesson has a second part, this will be shown at the bottom of the page, if it doesn't have one, then just select a lesson from the navigation above.
